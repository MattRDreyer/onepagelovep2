Welcome to Shrug,

Managing projects within your team can be a stressful process, keeping track of all the tasks and ensuring everyone is on the right path.

Shrug is a simple app that is designed to make it easy to manage projects by breaking them up into tasks which are organised by where they are in the pipeline; Completed, In-Progress, In-Review and Upcoming. You can invite your whole team, assign tasks to members, have discussions on tasks and overall remove the stress from project management.
